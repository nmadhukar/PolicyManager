## Definition Of Ready

- Goal is clear.
- Phase is known.
- Scope and non-goals are explicit.
- Acceptance criteria are testable.
- Security/RBAC impact is stated.
- Documentation impact is stated.
- Verification commands are known or marked unavailable because scaffold is not present yet.
